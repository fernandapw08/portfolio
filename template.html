Projects

Foodtruck Finder App

During the Google UX design certification program, I developed this project. The app seeks to help Foodtruck users find food trucks around their area, prices, menus, and contact information. Furthermore, some users may find helpful the ability to book a food truck for an event. On the other, hand the app pretends to help food truck owners to expand their businesses.
